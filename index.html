Our Story

A Taste of
Addis Ababa

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Addis Ababa, Wein Café & Restaurant is more than just a dining destination — it's a celebration of Ethiopian culinary heritage infused with modern elegance.

From our signature Ethiopian coffee ceremony, using single-origin beans from the highlands of Yirgacheffe, to our carefully curated menu that blends traditional flavors with contemporary presentation — every visit to Wein is an experience for the senses.
